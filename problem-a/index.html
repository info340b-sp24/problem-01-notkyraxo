Gojo Satoru

Gojo Satoru is a character from the popular anime series, Jujutsu Kaisen. In the series, he is the main protagonist's (Yuji Itadori) teacher and mentor. His abilities include Limitless Technique, Hollow Purple, and Domain Expansion. He is known as one of the strongest special grade Jujutsu sorcerors due to his possession of the Six Eyes.
